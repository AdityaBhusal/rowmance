# 🎭 Rowmance 

> **"Where every seat finds its soulmate."**

**Rowmance** is a boutique theatre ticketing and seating management system designed to make the journey from the lobby to the front row a total love story. Forget clunky, clinical spreadsheets—Rowmance treats every booking like a grand opening.

---

## 🌹 The Vision
In the world of theatre, the "Row" matters. It’s where first dates happen, where standing ovations begin, and where the magic of the stage meets the comfort of the audience. **Rowmance** was built to handle the "behind-the-scenes" drama of inventory and availability so that the only drama left is the one under the spotlights.

---

## 🎬 Act I: Features (The Playbill)

* **The Matchmaker:** A visual, interactive seating chart that helps users find their perfect "row-mate" (be it a front-row view or a cozy balcony nook).
* **No Heartbreak Bookings:** Real-time seat locking to prevent the ultimate tragedy: the double-booking.
* **VIP Treatment:** Support for tiered pricing—from "Orchestra Gold" to "Budget-Friendly Balcony."
* **Curtain Call Analytics:** A sleek dashboard for managers to track ticket sales and "Full House" status at a glance.
